# Welcome to SnapTranslator

**Translate the world instantly!**

SnapTranslator is a revolutionary tool that allows you to translate text from images or your camera in real time.  

### Key Features:
- Instant text recognition from images or camera  
- Translate to any language instantly  
- Save your translation history automatically  
- Smart suggestions for phrases and terms  
- Easy sharing of translations  

Whether you are a traveler, student, or business professional, SnapTranslator makes understanding foreign text effortless.
